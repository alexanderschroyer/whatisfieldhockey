## Reading this timeline

Dates and “firsts” are simplified. For museum displays, school papers, or press releases, cross-check the **FIH**, **Olympic** archives, **USA Field Hockey**, and national federation histories. For how the game is played today in the US (including **NCAA** modifications), use the [Rules of Hockey hub](https://www.usafieldhockey.com/rules-of-hockey) rather than this timeline.

The entries below emphasize **global** milestones and a **US angle** useful when explaining the sport to newcomers in Alabama.
