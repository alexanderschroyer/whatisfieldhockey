## Framing

There is **no single ladder** that guarantees an Olympic cap. Pathways blend **national team selection**, **club or college play**, **international competition exposure**, and **coach development priorities**. USA Field Hockey publishes current program names and tryout information — **replace any detail below** with their live pages when you share links.

## Youth and junior national teams

Age-group teams (labels change over cycles) identify players through camps, regional competition, and scouting. **Boys and girls** may have different camp calendars and international opportunities depending on the cycle and budget.

## Senior national teams

**Women’s** senior programming often intersects with the **NCAA calendar** for US-based athletes; **men’s** senior programming may pull from a **smaller domestic pool** and more international club experience. Both require extraordinary fitness, tactical literacy, and consistency against world-class opponents.

## Professional and club experience abroad

Many US senior-team athletes gain minutes in **European or other leagues** where the sport is deeper. Visa, contract, and national-team release rules are part of that life — not something this site details.
